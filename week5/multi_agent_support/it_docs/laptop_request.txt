Laptop Request Process

To request a new laptop:

1. Submit a request through the IT Service Portal: https://it.company.com/requests
2. Select "Hardware Request" > "Laptop"
3. Fill out the form with:
   - Reason for request (new hire, replacement, upgrade)
   - Preferred specifications (if any)
   - Urgency level
   - Manager approval

Standard Laptop Options:
- Dell Latitude 5540 (Standard business laptop)
- MacBook Pro 14" (For design/development roles)
- MacBook Air 13" (Lightweight option)

Processing Time:
- Standard requests: 5-7 business days
- Urgent requests: 2-3 business days (requires manager approval)

New Hire Process:
- Laptops are automatically ordered for new hires
- IT will contact you on your start date
- Setup assistance available on first day

Replacement Process:
- Submit request with reason (damage, performance issues, end of life)
- Include laptop serial number if available
- IT will assess and approve replacement

For questions, contact: it-hardware@company.com

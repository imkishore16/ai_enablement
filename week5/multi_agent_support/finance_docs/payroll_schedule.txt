Payroll Processing Schedule

Pay Periods:
- Bi-weekly payroll (every 2 weeks)
- Pay dates: Every other Friday

2025 Pay Schedule:

January:
- Pay Period 1: Jan 1-14, Paid: Jan 17
- Pay Period 2: Jan 15-28, Paid: Jan 31

February:
- Pay Period 1: Jan 29 - Feb 11, Paid: Feb 14
- Pay Period 2: Feb 12-25, Paid: Feb 28

March:
- Pay Period 1: Feb 26 - Mar 11, Paid: Mar 14
- Pay Period 2: Mar 12-25, Paid: Mar 28

Processing Timeline:
- Timesheets due: Monday by 5 PM (end of pay period)
- Payroll processed: Tuesday-Wednesday
- Direct deposit: Friday (or next business day if Friday is holiday)

Holiday Adjustments:
- If pay date falls on a holiday, payment is made on the previous business day

Direct Deposit:
- Set up through Employee Self-Service Portal
- Changes must be submitted by Wednesday of pay week
- Takes effect the following pay period

Pay Stub Access:
- Available on Employee Portal: https://portal.company.com/payroll
- Emailed to your company email on pay day

For payroll questions: payroll@company.com

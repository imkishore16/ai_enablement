Reimbursement Policy

Eligible Expenses:
- Business travel (flights, hotels, meals)
- Client entertainment (with prior approval)
- Professional development courses and conferences
- Office supplies (if not available through company procurement)
- Internet and phone expenses (for remote workers)

Reimbursement Process:

1. Submit Expense Report:
   - Use the Finance Portal: https://finance.company.com/expenses
   - Upload all receipts (must be itemized)
   - Include business purpose for each expense
   - Submit within 30 days of expense date

2. Approval Workflow:
   - Expenses under $100: Auto-approved
   - Expenses $100-$500: Manager approval required
   - Expenses over $500: Manager + Finance approval required

3. Processing Time:
   - Approved expenses: 5-7 business days
   - Payment via direct deposit to your registered bank account

Required Documentation:
- Original receipts (scanned copies accepted)
- Business purpose clearly stated
- Date, amount, and vendor information
- For travel: itinerary and conference/meeting details

Non-Reimbursable:
- Personal expenses
- Alcohol (unless client entertainment with approval)
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- Expenses without receipts

For questions: finance@company.com
